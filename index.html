Ingridients:

  • Two eggs
  • Chicken breast
  • Avocado
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. pour a small quantity if olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken breast in it.
  2. Add as much salt as desired to the chicken
  3. Take the chicken out of the pan ans take as many oil of it as posible
  4. Fry the eggs in the same oil to preserve the taste of the chicken
  5. Add pepper and salt to the eggs as desire
  6. Serve the eggs over the chiken
  7. cut the avocado and serve over the egs in a fashionable manner
